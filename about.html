Emil Matsakh

I'm the Founder and Managing Partner of Catalyst-DNA. Before starting this firm, I served as Chief Analytics Officer at Commonwealth Bank of Australia—one of the largest and most data-intensive financial institutions in the world. That role gave me something most consultants don't have: a decade of operating experience at the intersection of data strategy, enterprise architecture, and executive accountability.

I've sat in the room where data investments are approved and rejected. I've led the team that had to deliver on promises made to the board. I've navigated the politics of enterprise transformation, the complexity of regulatory compliance, and the engineering realities of large-scale data platforms. That context shapes every engagement we take on.

Why I started Catalyst-DNA

After years inside financial institutions, I saw the same patterns repeated across the industry: ambitious data strategies that stalled in implementation, expensive platforms that generated dashboards nobody used, and AI initiatives that produced prototypes but never reached production. The gap wasn't in the technology—it was in the connection between strategic intent and engineering execution.

Catalyst-DNA exists to close that gap. We're not a traditional consultancy that hands off a roadmap and disappears. We're not a pure engineering shop that builds without strategic context. We're a single partner that holds the full arc—from the "what and why" to the "how" to the "running it in production"—and stays accountable to outcomes throughout.

The Catalyst-DNA approach

We work with a small number of clients at any time, by design. Senior involvement isn't a pitch—it's structural. Every engagement is led by someone who has solved this problem before at scale. We move fast because we've seen the patterns. We ask better questions because we know where bodies are buried.

Our engagements are built around three phases:

  • Strategy: Define your data north star, audit current state, and build a prioritized roadmap tied to measurable business outcomes—not abstract maturity frameworks.
  • Build: Architect and engineer the platforms, models, and pipelines that your strategy demands. Modern stack. Production-grade. Designed for the regulatory and governance requirements of financial services.
  • Run: Operate the capability over time—monitoring, iterating, and scaling—so the value compounds rather than decays after the engagement ends.

Domain expertise

Catalyst-DNA is built specifically for financial services and adjacent sectors. Banking, insurance, asset management, fintech, real estate finance—these are industries where data quality, compliance, and model governance are not optional. We've built careers in these environments and we understand the constraints as well as the opportunities.

Beyond the work

I'm based in New York City. Outside of Catalyst-DNA, I serve as a strategic advisor on several ventures at the intersection of data and consumer technology. I'm also active in the New York tech and fintech community.

If you're thinking about a data or AI challenge—or just want to talk through an idea— I'm always open to a conversation. No pitch, no pressure.
